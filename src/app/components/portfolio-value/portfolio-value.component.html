Positions Value Over Time

The first line chart shows the position value over time of all the ETFs. The position value of an ETF is equal to the price of the ETF multiplied by the quantity of ETFs at a certain date. The deviations in the line chart are due to transactions involving the buying or selling of ETFs and changes in ETF prices.

Formula: position𝑖 = price 𝑖 x quantity𝑖, where 𝑖 is a certain date

The second line chart shows the position value of the entire portfolio by summing up the values of all ETFs from the first line chart.

The third line chart illustrates the cash flow, representing the money remaining at hand after buying or selling ETFs over time.

The last line chart combines the position value over time and cash on hand over time by adding them together.
